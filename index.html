Dear Riri

Have you ever wondered about moon and the waves?. They push and pull each other. At times they’re so low that it causes droughts in places, At times they’re so high that it becomes destructive. But Despite all that, They still stay in harmony. You’re my moon and I’m your waves and Vice Versa. Ofcourse, We’ll be closer than ever and at some instances we were never more apart. But that doesn’t mean I’ll abandon you. Stop caring for you. We’re human beings with ever-changing emotions but one thing I’m certain is my love for you is constantly evergreen and

growing.

Will you be my valentine?
